VEHICLE PAGE / HISTORY UPDATE

- Clicking a vehicle row or registration now opens a read-only vehicle record first.
- Vehicle record shows registration, vehicle, mileage, MOT due, tax due and linked customer.
- MOT and tax cards show clear good / due soon / overdue status.
- Full history is grouped into Job Sheets, Invoices and Service Sheets.
- Each history item can be opened directly.
- Vehicle details can still be amended using the dedicated Edit Vehicle Details button.
- The + Vehicle button continues to create a new vehicle.
