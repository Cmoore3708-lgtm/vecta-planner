VECTA PLANNER - ONE-TIME VERCEL SETUP

In Vercel, open the vecta-planner project:
Settings > Environment Variables

Add these two variables for Production, Preview and Development:

VITE_SUPABASE_URL
Value: your Supabase Project URL

VITE_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY
Value: your Supabase Publishable key beginning sb_publishable_

Do NOT use the Supabase secret key.

After adding them, redeploy the latest deployment. Every computer will then connect automatically.
