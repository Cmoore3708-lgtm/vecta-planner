VECTA PLANNER UPDATE — 20 JUL 2026

1. Drag to Unallocated Jobs
- Preserves the booking date and time.
- Sets Technician to Unallocated.
- Sets Ramp to Unallocated.
- Keeps the existing booked/status value.
- Avoids clearing database fields that may be required by Supabase.

2. Planner drag and resize stability
- Cloud refresh is suspended for the full duration of a drag or resize interaction.
- This prevents the three-second cloud refresh from rebuilding the planner midway through resizing.
- A move or resize is rendered only after the Supabase save finishes.
- Failed saves restore the original job position or duration.
