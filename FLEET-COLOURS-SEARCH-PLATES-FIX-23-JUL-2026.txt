FLEET MANAGER v4 — 23 JULY 2026

Changes:
- Contractor names beginning with WPC are automatically consolidated under WPC.
  This migrates existing browser-saved data, including WPC ALAN, WPC JAKE and WPC KEV.
- Each contractor is displayed with a consistent colour-coded badge.
- Fleet search now searches registration, vehicle, contractor/fleet, email, department and asset number.
  Multi-word searches are supported and a clear button has been added.
- Registration numbers now use the same yellow UK number plate with blue GB strip used elsewhere in the website.
- Vehicle profile headers also display the standard registration plate and contractor colour.
