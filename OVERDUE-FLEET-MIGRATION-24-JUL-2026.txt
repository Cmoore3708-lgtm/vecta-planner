OVERDUE FLEET SCHEDULE MIGRATION — 24 JULY 2026

Applied across every Fleet Manager maintenance plan and vehicle:
- Any active schedule with a current due date before 1 July 2026 is treated as completed.
- A completion-history record is added using the original overdue due date as the completion date.
- The new due date keeps the same day and month and moves to the next future year (currently 2027).
- Dates from 1 July 2026 onward are not changed.
- The migration also updates data already saved in the browser, not only the imported seed data.
- The migration is idempotent, so refreshing/reopening the app does not create duplicate completion records.
